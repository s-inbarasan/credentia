CREDENTIA

Where Identity Meets Defense

What is CREDENTIA?

CREDENTIA is an AI-powered cybersecurity web app designed to teach users how to stay safe online through interactive learning, real-world practice simulations, and intelligent AI assistance.

The Problem We Solve

Most people use weak passwords, easily fall for sophisticated fake emails, and lack the fundamental knowledge required to protect their digital identities from modern cyber threats.
